Ohio OVI Reference Guide

Pre-Investigation Planning

  • Review OVI indicators
  • Review SFST requirements
  • Review agency OVI policy
  • Consider crash-related factors
  • Consider drug impairment indicators
  • Review BMV 2255 procedures

Investigation Considerations

  • Driving behavior
  • Physical indicators
  • Statements
  • Field Sobriety Tests
  • Portable Breath Test (if authorized)
  • Drug impairment observations
  • Body camera documentation

Arrest & Processing

  • Establish probable cause
  • Read BMV 2255 as required
  • Determine test type
  • Document refusals
  • Inventory vehicle if applicable
  • Tow documentation
  • ALS documentation

Charging Review

  • OVI Alcohol
  • OVI Drugs
  • OVI Combined Influence
  • Open Container
  • DUS / FRA
  • Warrants
  • Drug Possession
  • Weapons Violations
  • Failure to Comply
  • Crash Related Offenses

Post-Arrest Checklist

  • Incident Report
  • OVI Narrative
  • Evidence Upload
  • Body Camera Review
  • Chemical Test Records
  • Tow Sheet
  • ALS Documentation
  • Court Packet
  • Prosecutor Materials
